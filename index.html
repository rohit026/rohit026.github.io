Installation Guide

Get started with SyncUp in just a few simple steps

1

Download the Extension

Click the button below to access the Google Drive folder and download the extension ZIP file.

📥 Download from Google Drive
2

Extract the ZIP File

Locate the downloaded ZIP file on your computer and extract it to a folder of your choice. Remember the location as you'll need it in the next step.

3

Open Chrome Extensions

Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/ or click the three-dot menu → Extensions → Manage Extensions.

4

Enable Developer Mode

Toggle the "Developer mode" switch in the top-right corner of the Extensions page to enable it.

5

Load Unpacked Extension

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder from Step 2. The SyncUp extension will now be installed and ready to use!

✨ You're all set! Open Google Meet and experience AI-powered meeting intelligence.
